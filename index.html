More about us

Welcome to our dance school, run by a highly trained Kathak dancer! We are dedicated to sharing the joy and beauty of Bollywood dance with students of all ages and skill levels. Our school offers classes for kids ages 5 and up, as well as adults. Our experienced instructors are passionate about teaching Bollywood dance and are committed to creating a fun and engaging learning environment for all our students. As a Kathak dancer, our instructor brings a unique perspective to Bollywood dance, incorporating classical Indian dance elements into the choreography. Students will learn a variety of dance styles, including traditional Bollywood, fusion, and contemporary, all while incorporating the intricate footwork and expressive movements of Kathak. In our classes, we focus on building a strong foundation in dance technique while also encouraging creativity and self-expression. We believe that dance is not just about learning the steps but also about cultivating a love for music, rhythm, and movement. Our students have the opportunity to showcase their skills in regular performances and cultural events. These performances not only allow students to share their love for dance with others but also build their confidence and stage presence. Join us at our Bollywood dance school and experience the joy of dance! Our classes are designed to be fun and accessible, regardless of your level of experience. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced dancer, our dedicated instructors will help you grow and achieve your dance goals.
